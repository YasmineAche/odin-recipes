Algerian Tamina

Description

Algerian tamina is a sweet semolina-based dessert made with butter, honey, and toasted semolina.

Itâ€™s traditionally prepared to celebrate births and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup medium or course semolina
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • Ground cinnamon to decorate.

Steps

  1. In a saucepan heat the semolina over a medium-high heat. Keep shaking the pan until the semolina is evenly toasted & a rich golden brown colour. Pour into a bowl & set aside.
  2. Melt the butter in the empty pan you used to toast the semolina. Remove from the heat & stir in the honey.
  3. Pour the semolina back into the pan & mix well to combine it with the butter & honey. If you notice the semolina mixture is too runny or has a little extra butter on top, just add a little extra semolina to the pan. If the semolina mixture is a little too stiff to pour, add 1 teaspoon extra of both the butter & the honey & mix well.
  4. Pour the semolina mixture out onto a small dinner plate or side plate & decorate with the cinnamon, silver balls & any additional items you like.
